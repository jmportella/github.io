Hello! I am Jhon Portella, a Ph.D candidate at UMBC. My research interests include nonlinear adaptive control, optimization and machine learning. I have a passion for research and development and exploring new algorithms, and I am dedicated to advancing automation technologies and robotics. Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my work, publications, and teaching experience.
